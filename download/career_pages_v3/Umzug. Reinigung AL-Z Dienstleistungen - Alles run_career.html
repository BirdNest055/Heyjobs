Europe & Canada's leading building & home trades marketplaces

The Instapro Group

We're the technology company powering the most successful building and home trades marketplaces in Europe and Canada.

Through our growing family of online platforms we provide a reliable way for tradespeople to get work they want and homeowners to hire the tradespeople they need.

We’re part of US market leader Angi Inc. and operate fast-growing platforms in the UK, France, Germany, Austria, Italy, the Netherlands and Canada.
