  • Office in Zürich

    Blending game development, R&D and administration, our headquarters in Schlieren, Switzerland are home to our management & business developers, level designers, engine programmers and event organizers. Located in the North of Switzerland, well-connected to major cities in Central Europe, our headquarters are close to the German office in Erlangen.

  • Office in Erlangen

    Located in the metropolitan region of Nuremberg, our office in Erlangen is the core of publishing, marketing & PR activities - including community management, graphic design and video production with a state-of-the-art streaming studio. Also found here: software related divisions like gameplay programming, sound design, web development and quality assurance as well as HR.

  • Office in Brno

    Located in the second-largest city in the Czech Republic after Prague, our office in Brno is primarily focused on 3D art. Our talented game artists are experts for vehicle and character creation, character animation, as well as map assets. They enrich the world of the game for an authentic farming experience and create authentic models based on real-life vehicles and tools.

Working at GIANTS

Working at GIANTS means working together - world-wide, and no matter the department or position. Instead of managing ourselves into dead ends, our passionate employees look for collaborative ways to grow themselves - individually, as a team, as a company. That’s why you find enthusiastic and open-minded people in every department and every single office, happy to work with you.

Job Description

To strengthen our business development team, we are looking for a


Junior License Coordinator

based in Erlangen, Germany.

You will support the management of licensing partnerships and ensure smooth coordination of all related processes. You will work closely with the Account Manager and contribute to maintaining strong relationships with international partners through structured communication, documentation, and operational support.


Job Responsibilities

  • Support the coordination and administration of international licensing partnerships
  • Maintain and update partner records, agreements, and communication in internal systems
  • Assist with day-to-day partner communication via email, phone, and occasional meetings
  • Prepare documents, reports, and materials related to licensing activities
  • Ensure all licensing data and documentation are accurate, complete, and well-structured
  • Coordinate internal and external stakeholders to ensure smooth collaboration
  • Support the team during industry events, trade shows, and partner visits (Germany & abroad)


Expected Qualifications

  • Initial experience in administration, coordination, or account support, or strong motivation to grow in this area
  • Interest in agriculture or experience in an agriculture-related environment is a plus
  • Strong organizational skills and attention to detail
  • Proactive, reliable, and service-oriented mindset
  • Good communication skills and ability to collaborate with international partners
  • Fluent English and native-level German


What would be a plus?

  • Interest in video games
  • Good knowledge of MS Office tools (Word, Excel, Outlook)
  • Class B driver’s license
  • Portuguese or Spanish communication skills


What do we offer?

  • Variety: Challenging, creative projects with ample room for innovation
  • Work-life Balance: Flexible hours to accommodate effective working styles
  • Inclusivity: open, collaborative atmosphere with flat hierarchies & open doors
  • Company Growth: we’re a young, diverse, successful, and growing company
  • Modern Workspace: up-to-date hardware & software solutions
  • Top location: central office with excellent public transport links
  • Stability & Perks: Permanent employment, attractive salary, bonuses
  • Extras: Complimentary drinks, snacks, and fruit, team-building events

Job Description

As our Quality Assurance department is expanding, we are seeking a driven and enthusiastic


Game Tester

for our QA team in Erlangen, Germany.


You will closely work with your surrounding QA team and report to the QA Lead. Our QA team defines best practices to deliver a polished, immersive experience to our global community of players.

Job Responsibilities

  • Identify, analyse, and log bugs in our database with clear and detailed reports
  • Conduct functionality tests on game features across multiple console platforms
  • Verify bug fixes implemented by developers through regression and confirmation testing
  • Test user-created mods submitted by our player community
  • Investigate player-reported issues to assist in troubleshooting and resolution
  • Use creative problem-solving to find new and imaginative ways to break our games
  • Design test strategies for various aspects of the game, determining what needs testing and how to approach it
  • Apply industry best practices, research multiple sources of information, and leverage your expertise to improve testing methods
  • Analyse existing workflows and processes, drive improvements and knowledge-sharing across the QA team
  • Provide feedback on game design, suggesting improvements or refinements to the development team


Expected Qualifications

  • Some experience in a game development studio (internship, freelance, or other relevant roles)
  • A strong passion for video games
  • Ability to work independently and as part of a team
  • Strong organisational skills, with the ability to prioritize tasks and meet deadlines
  • Excellent communication skills and proficiency in English
  • Knowledge of Farming Simulator


What would be a plus?

  • Experience with Mantis bug tracking software
  • Familiarity with Sony and Microsoft platforms, as well as cross-platform gaming
  • A deep love for farming and racing games and experience with Farming and racing simulators
  • Knowledge of or a strong interest in agricultural machinery, cars and motorsports


What do we offer?

  • Variety: Challenging, creative projects with ample room for innovation
  • Work-life Balance: Flexible hours to accommodate effective working styles
  • Inclusivity: open, collaborative atmosphere with flat hierarchies & open doors
  • Company Growth: we’re a young, diverse, successful, and growing company
  • Personal Development: individual opportunities for professional growth
  • Modern Workspace: up-to-date hardware & software solutions
  • Top location: central office with excellent public transport links
  • Stability & Perks: Permanent employment, attractive salary, bonuses
  • Extras: Complimentary drinks, snacks, and fruit
