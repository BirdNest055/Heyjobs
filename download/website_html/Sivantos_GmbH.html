A new company

On March 1, 2019, Singapore-headquartered Sivantos Group joined forces with Lynge/Denmark-based Widex to form WS Audiology. Please visit wsa.com for access to all relevant information about our current company where we strive to unlock human potential by making wonderful sound part of everyone’s life.

 

Until January 2015, Sivantos Group was a Siemens business unit. That’s when we became an independent entity. Ever since, Sivantos has worked hard to reshape the industry in terms of innovation with cutting-edge products, services, and channels. Finally, in 2019, we merged with Widex to continue on our path as WS Audiology.
