Why Why Movilitas.Cloud?

Movilitas.Cloud is the comprehensive serialization software to manage your batch traceability and serialized packaging operations. From generating unique serial numbers to automating your workflows, this software empowers you to gain full control and visibility over your serialized manufacturing.

Easy to use: The modern, intuitive interface requires minimal training, so you can get started quickly - no lengthy implementations.

Seamless integration: Integrate your packaging lines, printers, ERP/WMS, MES, and regulatory systems in one centralized software solution for end-to-end control.

Secure and reliable infrastructure: Hosted across three geographically split data centers, Movilitas.Cloud ensures high availability and disaster recovery for your critical serialization data.

Benefits

  • Gain full serialization visibility by consolidating all data into one platform. Track every product across its lifecycle, quickly identify issues, initiate recalls, and ensure safety. Movilitas.Cloud streamlines serialization and strengthens your supply chain.
  • Achieve unparalleled batch traceability: Record the complete lineage and genealogy of your batches, from raw materials to finished goods. Quickly identify the origin of quality issues and pinpoint affected products to initiate targeted recalls, protecting your brand reputation and customer safety.
  • Ensure regulatory compliance with ease: Stay ahead of evolving serialization regulations worldwide. This serialization software seamlessly integrates with government reporting systems, enabling you to digitally submit compliance data and exchange encrypted information with supply chain partners.
