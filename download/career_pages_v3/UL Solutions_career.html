    At the center of safety is you.

    From day one, we’ve blazed a trail protecting the planet and everyone on it. We break things, burn things and blow things up. All in the name of safety science.

    That’s where you come in — because none of it could happen without you. It takes passion to protect people, problem-solving to safeguard personal data and conviction to make the world a sustainable place. 

    It takes bold ideas and brilliant minds to build a better world for future generations across the globe.

     

     

    Create a safer world

    This is more than a job. It’s a calling. A passion to use our expertise to help keep people around the globe safe. To play our part in creating a more secure, sustainable world today — and tomorrow. As a member of the UL Solutions safety science community, you’ll use your ideas, your energy and your ambition to innovate, challenge and ultimately help create a safer world.

     

     

    Deliver work that matters​

    We’re a leading world expert in safety science. That means we’re at the forefront of the industry, combining cutting-edge technology and bold ideas to make a positive impact. Our teams have influenced billions of products, plus services, software offerings and more. UL Solutions has been pioneering change since 1894 and we’re still leading the way.

     

     

    Learn today to shape tomorrow

    Empowering our customers to keep the world safe means thinking ahead. It means having the power to grow our skills and deepen our knowledge every single day. It means investing in training and empowering our people to learn and innovate. At UL Solutions, we give our teams the tools to help build a better future — one where everyone benefits.

     

     

    Connect with global experts ​

    Everyone here is unique. But we’re also a global community, working together to help create a safer world. We tackle complex challenges together, share our ideas freely and develop deep and lasting bonds as a result. Join us and you can connect with the brightest minds in the business, all bringing their distinct perspectives and diverse backgrounds together to deliver real change.

     

     

    Total rewards

    UL Solutions total rewards programs vary by country and region. However, some benefits are universal. All UL Solutions employees are included in the company incentive bonus program, receive the equivalent of two paid days off each calendar year to volunteer for their community, and have access to learning and development opportunities. Additionally, we take a global approach to recognition.
