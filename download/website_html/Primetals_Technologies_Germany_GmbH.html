Primetals Technologies Germany

Company Overview

Primetals Technologies Germany GmbH is the headquarters for the Global Business Unit Electrics & Automation. With over 450 employees and six locations—including four offices and a dedicated test field—the company is a central force in advancing electrics, automation, and digital solutions for steel producers worldwide.

Business Information

Headquarters for Global Business Unit Electrics & Automation

Locations: Erlangen, Fürth, Oberhausen, Krefeld, Saarbrücken, Willstätt-Legelshurst

Employees: 450+

Competences and Locations

Primetals Technologies Germany delivers world-class electrics and automation expertise, supporting the metals industry with cutting-edge digitalization, electrification, project execution, and services.

Key Competences, Technologies, and Features

  • Digital solutions for downstream steel production
  • Mini-mill and electric arc furnace-based steelmaking expertise
  • Electrification and drive systems
  • Advanced electrics and automation for steelmaking and secondary metallurgy
  • Centralized project management, engineering, and test field capabilities
