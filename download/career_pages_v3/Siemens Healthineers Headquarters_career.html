Explore Varian, a Siemens Healthineers company 

Join us and let’s work together towards a world without fear of cancer.

Search jobs

Varian has long been on a mission to help create a world without fear of cancer. Now, as a Siemens Healthineers company, we are working as one, based on a more expansive perspective of the patient’s journey than ever before. As a result, our vision for the future – and what we can achieve on behalf of cancer patients – has expanded and become much more comprehensive.

Employer Awards

We continually raise the bar on our employee experience. The Great Place to Work® (GPTW) certification is an excellent recognition of this effort. In fiscal year 2024, over 80 percent of our employees worked in a certified Great Place to Work® country. Additionally, we have been recognized as one of the Best Workplaces™ in several countries, for example in Brazil, Canada, China, France, Germany, UK, and the USA.
