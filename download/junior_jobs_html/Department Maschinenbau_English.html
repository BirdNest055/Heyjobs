Researchers and Teaching Staff: From Idea to Innovation

Research at FAU stands for curiosity, openness and the courage to pursue new avenues. Interdisciplinary collaboration, suitable infrastructure and international networks create space for cutting-edge research. Various support services and programs are available to support research and teaching in all phases, from the initial idea, to implementation to knowledge transfer. FAU offers an environment where academic excellence, creativity and community thrive.
