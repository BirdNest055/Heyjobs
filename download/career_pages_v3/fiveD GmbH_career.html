Join fiveD!

fiveD is a fast-growing high-tech startup focused on radar simulation.
We develop a high-precision simulation platform that enables industry and research teams to design, test, and validate complex sensor systems in a realistic and scalable way.

Our work combines deep expertise in radar and simulation with modern software engineering and scalable compute infrastructure. We collaborate closely with industry partners and work on real-world projects in automotive, aerospace, and more.

We’re hiring across all experience levels – and if you’re excited about deep tech and real responsibility, you’ll fit right in. All open positions are hybrid/onsite in our HQ in Erlangen.

Apply now!

Field Application Engineer Radar Simulation (m/f/d)

Bridge complex radar simulation technology and clear external communication.
You’ll work closely with engineers to translate technical concepts into accurate and compelling content for customers, partners, and public audiences.
This includes presentations, technical marketing material, use-case descriptions, and support for demos and events.
Your work ensures that our technology is communicated clearly, credibly, and with the right technical depth.

Radar Application Engineer (m/f/d)

Work at the intersection of radar technology, simulation, and real customer applications. You’ll use our radar simulation platform to support application-specific customer projects, analyze concrete radar use cases, and generate meaningful technical insights.
In close collaboration with customers and internal engineering teams, you’ll translate requirements into tailored simulation setups and results.
Your work directly contributes to customer success and feeds back into the continuous improvement of our simulation platform.
