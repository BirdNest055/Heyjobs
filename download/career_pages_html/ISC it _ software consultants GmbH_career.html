Industries

we specialize in - for talent acquisition 

IT & Sales


Primarily Anglo Saxon stocklisted IT companies in cutting edge technologies and fast paced environments request our services year after year. Sales and IT candidates are our focus. 

Automotive


We place a special emphasis on recruiting  top engineers for positions such as for software development, functional safety, batteries, ADAS, Automated Driving. Our production engineers fully understand the art of increasing production efficiencies.

Banks & Finance

Outsourcing & Offshoring


For outsourcing companies we fill key positions in consulting, operations, delivery and sales. Since 1999 ISC developed a network to leading service delivery companies.
